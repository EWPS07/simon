Welcome to Simon

This is Simon, the classic 80's memory game. If you've ever played Simon before, you should find it very familiar.

Gameplay

To play Simon, turn it on, choose your difficulty, and if you want to play in strict mode, click the strict button. Simon will start building a sequence of colors and sounds for you to memorize. The number in the center will display the current count, which corresponds to the number of sounds in your sequence. Simon starts with 1 sound, if you respond correctly, Simon will play back the previous sound, plus a new one. Simon will repeat this process until you cannot complete the sequence, or until you beat Simon.

Difficulty

In this version of Simon, there are 3 levels of difficulty, easy(E), medium(M), and hard(H). For easy, to achieve a win, an 11 move sequence must be memorized, for medium, a 20 move sequence, and for hard, a 32 move sequence.

Strict Mode

In addition to the levels of difficulty, we also have strict mode. Strict mode only allows 1 attempt at completing the given sequence. If a wrong move is made, it's game over. Simon will restart at 1. If you are not in strict mode, Simon will replay the current sequence over for you until you get it correct.
